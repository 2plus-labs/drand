drand

A Distributed Randomness Beacon

Overview

The drand randomness beacon is a world-wide network of independent partners who provide verifable, high-entropy randomness as a service to the public. To generate this randomness in an unbiasable and unpredictable way, the drand network relies on cutting-edge methodologies from cryptography and distributed systems research. For more details on the technology, please refer to the documentation.
